Hi, here is a little bit about me...

After my undergraduate degree in Accounting and Finance, I was all set to work in banking as a credit analyst and work my way up to a CFA career. All that changed when I was introduced to programming by a friend. I loved programming and creating something concrete that people could use everyday. I knew I had to swtich career so I self taught Java programming and entered the workforce as a Software Engineer at Accenture. I worked in various projects for over 6 years as a software developer focusing on front-end and back-end web development. I worked on all phases of the software lifecycle on the projects and took on lead roles.

I commit myself one hundred percent to everything I do. That is why after almost a seven year stint at Accenture I made a decision to take a break from work when my son was born. Now after 5 years and two children later, I am ready to get back into the workforce and continue to pursue my passion.

As a way to brush up on my coding skills and update myself on new technologies, I have created this resposive website from scratch and included other projects I worked on recently. I have also taken many relevant courses online to get me up to speed.
